Luxury Pixel Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 26, 2026

Luxury Pixel is a Wear OS watch face for Pixel Watch and other compatible Wear OS devices. This policy explains how the watch face handles data.

Data Collection

Luxury Pixel does not collect, store, sell, share, or transmit personal data. The watch face does not include analytics, advertising SDKs, account login, server communication, or third-party tracking.

On-Device Watch Information

Luxury Pixel may display information provided locally by Wear OS, such as the time, date, step count, battery level, and heart rate. This information is used only to render the watch face on your device and is not sent to the developer or to any external service by Luxury Pixel.

Health and Fitness Information

The watch face may show activity and fitness values, including steps and heart rate, when those values are made available by the device. Luxury Pixel is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, monitoring, or treatment.

Children's Privacy

Luxury Pixel is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated if the watch face changes. The effective date above will reflect the latest version.

Contact

For privacy questions about Luxury Pixel, contact the developer through the contact information listed on the Google Play store listing.
